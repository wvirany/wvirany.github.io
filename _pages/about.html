I recently graduated from the University of Colorado with a B.S. in applied mathematics, as well as minors in computer science and physics. Broadly speaking, I am interested in machine learning, statistics, and optimization, typically with applications to the life sciences.

Specifically, I am currently working on a project involving the validation of generative AI models used in molecular design for early drug discovery. AI is poised to revolutionize the drug development industry and make the discovery of new therapeutics faster and more affordable. Additionally, I am interested in precision medicine. With recent advancements in DNA sequencing technologies, there is an opportunity to develop our understanding of the role of the human microbiome in overall health by leveraging advanced statistical and machine learning techniques.
