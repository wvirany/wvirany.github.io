I'm a student at the University of Colorado Boulder concurrently completing my B.S and M.S. in applied mathematics. My general interests lie in machine learning and deep learning; both theory and applications, as well as statistics and optimization.

Specifically, I am investigating the generalization properties of overparameterized models; i.e. models which can perfectly fit noisy training data, yet still perform well on unseen test data. I am also interested in the application of machine learning to the metagenomic analysis of the human gut microbiome. Models which can predict disease earlier and more accuractely have the potential to revolutionize P4 medicine (personalized, preventive, predictive, participatory).
